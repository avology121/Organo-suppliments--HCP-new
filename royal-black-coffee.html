When is the best time to drink coffee?

Coffee is a very versatile drink, however, due to its caffeine concentrations, the best time to drink it is thought to be 9:30–11:30 a.m. when most people’s cortisol level is low. You can also choose to drink it 30-60 minutes before exercising. Caffeine can stay in the body up to 5 hours.

​Is it true that 2-3 cups of coffee a day is good for the body/health?

According to some studies, the ideal number of cups you can drink a day to get the cardiovascular benefits of coffee is between 2 and 3 cups a day. People who kept their consumption in this range showed fewer heart problems and tended to have greater health.
